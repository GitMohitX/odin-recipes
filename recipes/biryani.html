Short Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 2 cups Basmati rice, rinsed and soaked
  • 500g chicken pieces, cut into large chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1-2 tsp red chili powder (to taste)
  • 2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (e.g., 1 bay leaf, 3-4 cardamom pods, 2-3 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick)
  • 4 cups water or chicken stock
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish
  • Fried onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, half of the biryani masala, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© whole spices and sliced onions in hot oil/ghee until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook until browned and well combined.
  4. Stir in drained rice, pour in water or stock, and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked and water absorbed.
  6. Let rest for 5 minutes, then fluff gently. Garnish with fresh herbs and optional fried onions.
