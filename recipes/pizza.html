Quick Mini Pizzas

A fast and easy recipe for delicious mini pizzas using tortilla bases. Perfect for a quick snack or light meal!

Ingredients

  • Mini tortillas or pre-made pizza bases
  • Pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Mini pepperoni slices (optional)
  • Toppings of choice (e.g., bell peppers, olives, onions)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Lay the tortillas on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce on each tortilla, leaving a small border.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings and place them in the oven.
  6. Bake for 8-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy and golden brown.
  7. Remove carefully from the oven, let cool slightly, and serve hot!

Enjoy your homemade mini pizzas!
